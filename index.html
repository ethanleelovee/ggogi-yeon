Our Story

Fire is
ritual.

"Every cut tells a story — of the land, the craft, the hands that raised it."

Born from a love of Korean tradition and Melbourne's relentless culinary spirit, GGOGI YEON brings the ancient ritual of table-side grilling to the heart of the city.

We source premium beef from Victoria's finest farms — dry-aged, hand-sliced, served with eight rotating banchan. Every visit is a performance: flame, smoke, and the quiet joy of gathering.

The Experience

Three elements.
One evening.

01

Premium Cuts

Wagyu, Black Angus, and heritage pork — sourced from Victoria's finest farms. Every piece hand-selected and dry-aged in-house.

02

Charcoal Grills

Binchotan white charcoal, set into each table. The cleanest burn, the deepest flavour. You cook it, you own it.

03

Banchan Culture

An ever-changing selection of eight house-made side dishes — kimchi, japchae, seasoned vegetables — served with every meal.
